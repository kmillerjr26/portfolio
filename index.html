My UX Skills

User Research

The best design decisions stem from research. I seek to uncover the user's needs, motivations, and pain points by asking them questions about what they say, do, feel, and think, and synthesizing their answers to spot patterns and trends. This helps me to define the problem at hand.

Information Architecture

I believe it's important for people to navigate a digital product and find what they're looking for with ease, so I think of ways to group and structure the product's pages in a way that's intuitive for them to understand.

Prototyping

From grayscale wireframes to clickable prototypes, I create seamless interactive experiences using tools like Figma and Adobe XD, and use my front-end development knowledge to bridge the gap between design and coding.

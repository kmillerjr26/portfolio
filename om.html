01. Brand Philosophy

Meditation is proven to do a lot for the human body spiritually, emotionally, and mentally. In today’s world, it’s easy for many to get caught in the stress of daily life. This is where Om comes in; this app brings the yoga studio to you, wherever you are, to provide you with a high-quality meditation experience. We understand knowing where to start can be a major roadblock for those new to meditation, so Om is tailored to offer meditation classes for all levels.

Our Goals for the App

  • Make meditation a regular practice in users’ lives
  • Become a social hub for anyone interested in wellness and meditation practices
  • Partner with local businesses to promote their meditation and wellness classes while increasing our brand awareness

02. Where Do We Start?

We started to scope out our competition and created a competitive analysis to get a better understanding of the current meditation app market. Our main competitor would be Headspace, another app centered around instructor-led guided meditation with daily meditation reminders. They're recognized as one of the leading mindfulness and meditation apps out on the market, so we started to think about how to differentiate our brand from the competition.

Next, we interviewed six people, mostly young adults in their 20's-30's, and conducted a survey to get a gauge on what users are looking for when they’re using a meditation app. We found our interviewees struggled with finding the time and motivation for meditation, but were mostly open to incorporating it in their lives to be more present amidst their busy lives.

We also created a mood board to get our ideas organized. Interestingly, our final product turned out to be much different than where our initial thought process started.


03. What Does Om Offer?

After our research process, we began generating ideas for what Om could offer to users. The features we wanted to include were:

  • Video-based guided meditations with multiple teachers to choose from
  • Rewards for when users reach milestones
  • Community forum board to meet other people and create/respond to threads on meditation topics
  • Search for in-person meditation classes; we’d want to partner with Mindbody to show classes from their database in our search system

Problem Statement

While there are a myriad of apps that promote meditation and yoga, none of them provide users with the opportunity to customize a yoga or meditation routine based around the user’s needs, interests, and skill level.

Om is here to help people find time to relax and stay centered no matter how stressful or hectic life can get. Om is a revolutionary application that places the user in control of their mental health and well being. Users can select their level of experience with yoga and meditation, collect new achievement badges, and try various types of meditations to see which ones work best for them and their needs.

Users will not only have virtual guided meditation but the app will also help users find in-person meditation and yoga classes.

We created a user flow from here to serve as the basis for our low fidelity prototype.

  • What will keep users coming back to this app daily?

About

My name is Kelly, and I'm a UX/UI designer.

My interest in user-centered design comes from a desire to understand people and what they need, and the challenge that arises to meet those needs using design and interactivity. As a curious, analytical person at heart, I’m constantly seeking opportunities to broaden my design knowledge and skillset. I hold a B.A. in Graphic Design from George Mason University and a UX/UI certficiation from George Washington University's College of Professional Studies.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn. You can also send me an email at kmillerjr26@gmail.com. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio site.

My UX Skills Include:

User Empathy

I seek to uncover the user's needs, motivations, and pain points by asking them questions about what they say, do, feel, and think, and synthesizing their answers to spot patterns and trends. This serves as my basis to define the problem at hand.

Information Architecture

I believe it's important for people to navigate a digital product and find what they're looking for with ease, so I think of ways to group and structure the product's pages in a way that's intuitive for them to understand.

Interaction Design

Using my strong background in visual design, I aim to create seamless interactive experiences that engage others.

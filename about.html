User Empathy

I seek to uncover the user’s needs, motivations, and pain points by asking them questions about what they say, do, feel, and think, and synthesizing their answers to spot patterns and trends. This serves as the basis to define the problem at hand.

Information Architecture

I believe it's important for users to efficiently navigate the pages of a digital product and find what they're looking for with ease. This involves grouping, organizing, and structuring the product's navigation elements in a way that's intuitive for users to understand.
